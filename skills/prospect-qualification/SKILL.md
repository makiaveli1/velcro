# Skill: Prospect Qualification Rubric

Use when qualifying a lead, reviewing a prospect, or assessing whether a deal is worth pursuing.

## BANT-adjacent Qualification

### Budget
- Do they have budget for the proposed solution?
- Is the budget real or theoretical?
- What's the typical deal size for a company of their size?

### Authority
- Who makes the final decision?
- Are you talking to that person?
- What's the decision-making process?

### Need
- Is there a clear, articulated problem?
- Is the problem urgent enough to act?
- Have they tried to solve it before?

### Timeline
- When do they want to solve this by?
- Is there a hard deadline or just "sometime"?
- What happens if they don't solve it in that window?

## Red Flags
- No clear budget
- No clear decision-maker
- "Just exploring" without commitment
- Waiting for something external (funding, restructuring, etc.)
- Trying to use your work as leverage with their current vendor

## Green Flags
- Specific problem with specific stakes
- Active search or expressed urgency
- Decision-maker in the room
- Realistic timeline and budget

## Output Format
Verdict: Qualified / Needs More / Not a Fit + reasoning per BANT category.
Be direct. Don't qualify a lead that doesn't qualify.
