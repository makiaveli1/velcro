# Website Studio — Positioning

## What This Is

**A pre-sales opportunity discovery and concept studio system.**

Not a web design agency. Not a cold outreach service. Not another "we build websites" shop.

The core value proposition: *identify businesses with real website opportunity gaps, research them deeply, and produce a custom website concept direction that demonstrates exactly what they'd get — before asking for anything.*

This is the difference between:
- **Agency pitch:** "Hi, we build websites, want one?"
- **This system:** "We noticed [Business X] appears to be losing [Y] because of [specific gap]. Here's what that could look like solved. Talk to us if it resonates."

The concept-first approach means every outbound interaction starts with demonstrated value, not a cold ask.

---

## The Angle

**"Website opportunity identification + custom concept preview"**

The primary framing is NOT "we'll build your website." It's "we find businesses whose web presence is costing them opportunities and show them exactly what's possible."

Secondary framing by context:
- Businesses with no site: "You exist online in 2026 as a business card and a Google Maps pin. Here's what that gap is costing you."
- Businesses with outdated sites: "Your site signals 2014. Your competitors are signalling 2024. Here's what modern looks like for your market."
- Businesses with mismatched sites: "Your business is more serious than your website shows. Here's what credibility online actually looks like."

---

## What We Do

1. **Find** businesses with genuine website opportunity gaps
2. **Research** them deeply — understand their market, their competitors, their customers, their story
3. **Audit** their current site if they have one (or build a brief if they don't)
4. **Design** a custom website concept direction — specific to them, not a template
5. **Pitch** with evidence and a concrete direction — not a brochure
6. **Queue** everything for human approval before any external action

## Offer Ladder

### Tier 1 — Website Opportunity Audit (Free, no commitment)
Visual audit + mobile review + performance check + trust signal assessment + 3 specific improvement recommendations.
Delivered as a structured document. No pitch attached — just genuine findings.

### Tier 2 — Homepage Concept Direction (Low-friction entry, ~€99–€199)
Custom mockup or detailed concept brief for their homepage — showing exactly what a modern, fit-for-purpose site could look like for their specific business and market.
Delivered with a brief rationale. No obligation.

### Tier 3 — Full Website Redesign/Build (Core offer, €799–€1,799)
Complete custom design and build. Based on the concept direction they already approved.
Includes mobile-first design, copy alignment, SEO foundations, trust architecture, and conversion structure.

### Tier 4 — Conversion-Focused Refresh (Add-on, €349–€599)
For businesses with an existing site that "works" but underperforms. Targeted improvements to conversion flow, trust signals, mobile UX, and messaging clarity.

### Tier 5 — Maintenance & Iteration (Ongoing, €99/month)
Monthly iteration, performance monitoring, content updates, A/B testing, SEO maintenance.

## Primary Offer Direction

**Lead with Tier 2 (Homepage Concept Direction) as the primary engagement hook.**

Reasoning:
- It's specific enough to demonstrate real value
- It's cheap enough to not require lengthy procurement
- It produces a tangible artifact the prospect can react to
- It naturally qualifies or disqualifies the lead without pressure
- It sets up Tier 3 as the logical next step, not a cold upsell

The pitch for Tier 2: *"We've looked at your business and your market, and we think your website could be working significantly harder for you. We've put together a concept direction — a concrete vision for what a modern homepage could look like for you. No obligation, no strings. If it resonates, we'll talk about what it would take to build it properly."*

## The Non-Negotiable Difference

Every business we approach has been researched. Every concept we show is custom. Every finding we share is specific and evidenced. We do not send templated emails. We do not pretend to have talked to them. We do not spam.

If we cannot produce a specific, credible, custom concept for a business, we do not pitch them.
