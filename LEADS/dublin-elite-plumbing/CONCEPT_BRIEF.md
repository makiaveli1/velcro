## Business Essence

Dublin Elite Plumbing is a 24/7 emergency plumbing service operating in Dublin 12 and surrounding areas. Established 2018. Known for fast response times, reliability, and professionalism. 89 Google reviews at 4.9 stars — customers consistently highlight speed and trustworthiness. Primary target: Dublin homeowners and small businesses who need a plumber fast.

**Primary service:** Emergency plumbing (burst pipes, leaks, blockages)
**Target customer:** Dublin 12 homeowners, landlords, small businesses — anyone with a plumbing emergency
**Key differentiator:** Speed + 4.9-star reputation on Google = the trustworthy emergency plumber choice

---

## The Core Website Problem

Every month, dozens of potential customers find Dublin Elite Plumbing on Google Maps, click through, and hit a wall: no website, no way to research the business, no pricing signals, no gallery of past work. They either call anyway (and hope) or move to the next result. A professional site turns those Google Maps clicks into booked jobs — and captures the non-emergency enquiries (bathroom renovations, boiler installs) that currently have nowhere to land.

## Design Language Direction

### Colour Palette
**Mood:** Authoritative, trustworthy, urgent without being alarming
**Direction:** Deep navy (#0f2d52) as primary — signals trust and professionalism; bright amber (#f59e0b) as accent — urgency, energy, Dublin warmth; off-white (#f8f9fa) base for cleanliness
**Why this palette fits:** Emergency plumbing needs to feel trustworthy and fast — not clinical, not overly corporate. Navy says competence; amber says urgency and warmth.

### Typography
**Type direction:** Clean sans-serif, humanist weight
**Recommended fonts:** "Plus Jakarta Sans" for headings — modern but warm; "Inter" for body — clean readability
**Why this:** A service business site needs to communicate quickly. Strong headings, scannable body copy. No decorative type that slows the reader down.

### Visual Tone
Trustworthy (not corporate-cold), fast/responsive (not formal), professional (not amateur-hour), approachable (not clinical).
**Imagery direction:** Real photography of plumber at work, van/equipment, completed bathroom installations. Warm, natural light. Not stock. Show the human side of the business.
**What NOT to feel like:** Corporate SaaS landing page. Generic WordPress plumbing template. Cold/clinical hospital aesthetic.

---

## Layout & Structure — Homepage

### Page Priority Order
1. **Hero — Phone CTA + "24/7 Emergency Plumber Dublin"** — Above the fold, always. A customer in crisis needs to find the phone number in under 3 seconds. No scrolling required.
2. **Services strip** — Emergency, Heating, Bathrooms, Installations — fast scanning for what they need
3. **"Why Dublin Elite" — trust signals** — 89 reviews, 24/7 availability, 30-minute response, established 2018
4. **Gallery** — Before/after of completed work — builds confidence fast
5. **Contact/About** — Location, phone, brief about section, map

### What NOT to have
No long service lists (they call for emergency, not a brochure). No blog/news (zero traffic to justify it). No multi-step forms (max friction for a customer in crisis).

### Trust Architecture
**Priority 1:** Phone number — always visible, top-right, prominent
**Priority 2:** "89 Google Reviews, 4.9 stars" — social proof above the fold
**Priority 3:** Trust badges — fully insured, licensed, established 2018

### CTA Model
**Primary CTA:** Phone call (tap-to-call on mobile — always working)
**Secondary CTA:** Contact form (for non-urgent enquiries)
**Mobile CTA:** Sticky phone button — bottom of screen, always visible on mobile

- Minimum 8–10 photos of completed work (bathrooms, pipework, heating installations)

Customer testimonials — 89 Google reviews but no written quotes they control. Recommend 3 short written testimonials from satisfied customers.
