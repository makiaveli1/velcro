# RESEARCH_BRIEF.md — Research Brief Runbook

_When Nero gives me a research question, do this._

---

## Step 1 — Understand the Question

Restate the question in my own words. Is it specific enough?  
If vague, ask Nero for clarification before starting.

Ask: what does a successful answer look like? What decisions will this inform?

---

## Step 2 — Define Scope

What is in scope? What is explicitly out of scope?

Time boundary: what is recent enough to be relevant?

---

## Step 3 — Search Strategy

Define the search approach before searching:
- Primary queries to run
- Key sources to check
- What "done" looks like for this brief

---

## Step 4 — Gather

Run searches. Collect sources. Assess quality.

For each source:
- Title, URL, date
- What it says
- Reliability: HIGH / MEDIUM / LOW
- Relevance to the question

---

## Step 5 — Synthesize

Organize findings into:
1. **Confirmed facts** — with sources
2. **Inferred conclusions** — labeled as such
3. **Unknowns** — what wasn't found or can't be confirmed

---

## Step 6 — Deliverable Format

```
## Research Brief: [question]

### Context
[restated question + why it matters]

### Findings

#### Confirmed
- [finding] — [source, date]

#### Inferred
- [finding] — [reason + confidence: medium/high]

#### Unknown
- [what we don't know]

### Analysis
[concise synthesis — what it means, patterns, implications]

### Sources
[numbered list of sources with reliability ratings]

### Confidence
HIGH / MEDIUM / LOW — [reason]
```

---

## Confidence Level

Assign an overall confidence level to the brief:

| Level | Meaning |
|---|---|
| **High** | Multiple confirmed sources, consistent findings |
| **Medium** | Some confirmed, some inferred, some gaps |
| **Low** | Limited sources, high uncertainty, significant unknowns |

---

## When to Escalate

If the question surfaces something that:
- Requires a business decision
- Is time-sensitive
- Has implications beyond the stated scope

Escalate to Nero immediately.
