## 1. ICP — Who We're Pitching

**Ideal Customer:**
Dublin-based trades business — electrician, plumber, painter, carpenter, heating engineer

**Criteria:**
- 2–15 employees (not solo, not big firm)
- Owner-managed (we deal directly with the decision maker)
- **Focus: Electricians and Plumbers first** (emergency services, high urgency, phone-driven leads)
- Existing website that looks outdated/neglected OR has a Facebook page instead of a site
- Gets work via word-of-mouth but has no real online presence
- Advertises anywhere (local paper, van, Word of Mouth) — means they understand marketing costs money
- Located in Dublin or surrounding counties

**Why these businesses:**
- Usually no in-house marketing person
- Owner makes the buying decision on instinct, no committee
- Often paying for Google Ads ineffectively — a better website fixes this
- They're used to spending €200–€500 on trade advertising — website feels same category

**Who to skip:**
- Big firms with a marketing department (won't deal with us)
- Solo operators who don't answer emails (no money, no organization)
- Businesses already paying a web agency (we're not cheaper, we're newer)

## 3. The Process (Step by Step)

**Step 1 — Prospect (done by me)**
- Find Dublin trades businesses with bad/old websites
- Note their current site URL, business name, phone, email
- Build a quick mockup for each one (HTML template customized with their name/services)

**Step 2 — Cold Email (done by me, sent from your Gmail)**
- Email 1: The hook — "We noticed your site, here's a mockup redesign" (sent Tue–Thu 8–10am)
- Email 2: Follow-up (Day 4) — same mockup, softer ask
- Email 3: Final (Day 10) — last chance, urgency

**Step 3 — Response / Reply**
- If they reply → we stop the sequence
- Gbemi responds personally, short and warm
- Ask for a 10-minute call to discuss

**Step 4 — Discovery Call (Gbemi does this)**
- 10-minute call: understand their business, what's working, what they want
- No hard pitch — ask good questions, show the mockup, give rough sense of price
- If ready: send a simple proposal (1-page PDF)
- If not ready: add to nurture list, follow up in 3 months

**Step 5 — Proposal**
- Simple 1-page proposal: what we're building, price, timeline, next steps
- Sent via email as PDF
- Give them 5 days to decide
- Follow up once if no response

**Step 6 — Deposit + Build**
- 50% deposit to start
- 50% on completion
- Gbemi builds the site (or outsources to a developer if needed)
