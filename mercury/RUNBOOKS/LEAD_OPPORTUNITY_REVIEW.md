# LEAD_OPPORTUNITY_REVIEW.md — Lead/Opportunity Review Runbook

_When reviewing a lead or opportunity, do this._

---

## What Is a Lead/Opportunity Review?

Taking a potential client or opportunity and assessing:
- Is this worth pursuing?
- What's the likelihood of converting?
- What's the potential value?
- What do we need to move forward?

---

## Step 1 — Gather the Facts

What do we know about the lead?
- Company / individual
- Role and background
- How they found us or how we found them
- What they said they need
- Budget signals (if available)
- Timeline signals

What do we NOT know — and is that a problem?

---

## Step 2 — Assess Fit

| Dimension | Score | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Problem fit — do we solve their actual problem? | HIGH / MED / LOW | |
| Budget fit — can they afford us? | HIGH / MED / LOW | |
| Timeline fit — do they need it when we can deliver? | HIGH / MED / LOW | |
| Strategic fit — does this advance Verdantia's direction? | HIGH / MED / LOW | |
| Relationship fit — do we have rapport or referrals? | HIGH / MED / LOW | |

---

## Step 3 — Rate the Opportunity

**Hot** — High fit across most dimensions. Worth prioritizing.
**Warm** — Good fit, some gaps or unknowns. Worth a conversation.
**Cold** — Low fit or too early. Deprioritize unless bandwidth allows.

---

## Step 4 — Recommended Next Step

What is the single best next action?
- Intro call?
- Proposal?
- Send specific resource?
- Decline politely?
- More research needed first?

---

## Step 5 — Deliverable Format

```
## Lead/Opportunity Review: [name/company]

### Facts Known
- [item]

### Facts Unknown
- [item]

### Fit Assessment
| Dimension | Score | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Problem fit | HIGH/MED/LOW | |
| Budget fit | HIGH/MED/LOW | |
| Timeline fit | HIGH/MED/LOW | |
| Strategic fit | HIGH/MED/LOW | |
| Relationship fit | HIGH/MED/LOW | |

### Overall Rating
HOT / WARM / COLD — [brief rationale]

### Recommended Next Step
[one specific action]

### Key Risks
- [what could go wrong]

### What I Need to Move Forward
[questions, research, or decisions needed]

### Confidence
HIGH / MEDIUM / LOW — [reason]
```

---

## Quality Rules

- Be honest about cold leads — don't pad warm ratings to be nice
- If we can't solve their actual problem, say so
- If the opportunity is not a fit, recommend declining early — saves everyone time
- Flag if there's a conflict of interest or ethical concern
