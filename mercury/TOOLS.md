## Commercial Reference Frames

When drafting for Verdantia:

**Audience:** Businesses needing AI consulting, training, enablement. Decision-makers with budget authority. Technical enough to understand AI value, not necessarily practitioners.

**Positioning:** Expert, practical, no-fluff. Not the cheapest. Likely the most credible and actionable.

**Voice:** Direct. Specific. Confident without being arrogant. Practical over aspirational.

---

## Draft Quality Checklist

Before delivering any draft to Nero:

1. Purpose stated — what is this trying to accomplish?
2. Audience clear — who is this for?
3. Call to action — what should the reader do?
4. Specific claims — are numbers, names, results real?
5. Honest framing — does this match what we can actually deliver?
6. Approved framing — does this align with Verdantia's positioning?
