## Campaign Brief

### Why This Segment — Tightened

Plumbers and electricians in Dublin are the right first target:
- High search volume when Dublin customers have emergencies or need quotes
- Very common to have no website or a 2010-era static page
- Clear customer journey: Google search → Google Maps → Phone call
- Phone CTA dominant — straightforward conversion model
- Trust signals (licences, insurance, certifications) are critical and often absent

**Painter/decorator, roofer, and HVAC trades are deferred to Round 2.** Round 1 stays narrow.

**Discipline: 5 sends maximum in Round 1. Not 10. Not "whatever is ready." Five.** We learn more from 5 excellent sends than 10 mediocre ones.

---

## Approach: Tier 1 First (Audit-Led)

For Round 1, lead with the free Website Opportunity Audit offer — not the paid concept.

**Rationale:**
- We are unproven. Businesses have no reason to trust us yet.
- Offering a free, specific, no-obligation audit is a much easier first ask than a concept pitch
- The audit demonstrates research quality and specific expertise before any commercial conversation
- It qualifies the lead naturally — if they don't engage with a free audit, they won't engage with a pitch
- It sets up a commercial conversation on evidence, not enthusiasm

**The Round 1 outreach offer:**
> "We've done a short opportunity audit of your web presence — three specific things we noticed, with observations on what's working and what might be costing you opportunities. Happy to share it — no commitment, no sales call."

**If they engage with the audit:** Nero reviews and decides whether to escalate to concept pitch in Round 2.
**If they don't engage:** suppress and move on.

## Phase 1 — Lead Discovery (Days 1–3)

### Who: Scout

**Action:** Run 2 discovery sessions:
- Dublin plumbers (Google Maps + TrueROI)
- Dublin electricians

**Method:**
1. Google Maps / Google Business Profile search by category + Dublin area
2. Quick score (5–8 min per lead) — score ≥ 41 to add
3. Add 10–15 leads to CRM
4. Mark AUDIT-WORTHY vs PITCH-WORTHY

**Output:** 10–15 leads in CRM/INDEX.md, lead folders with LEAD_RECORD.md

**Constraints:**
- Plumbers + electricians only in Round 1
- Dublin only
- No outreach during this phase
- Quality gate: could I write a specific opening hook for this business? If no, don't add it

---

## Phase 2 — Research and Audit (Days 3–7)

### Who: Scout (with Nero checkpoint at Day 5)

**Action:** Complete full research for top 5–8 leads:
- BUSINESS.md
- WEBSITE_AUDIT.md (existing site) or NO_SITE_BRIEF.md (no site)
- Score update if new information changes initial score

**Output:** 5–8 fully researched leads ready for concept phase

**Checkpoint (Day 5):** Nero reviews first 3 completed leads. If research is generic or thin, returns to Scout with specific feedback before more work is done.

---

## Phase 3 — Concept Briefs (Days 7–10)

### Who: Forge

**Action:** Produce CONCEPT_BRIEF.md for 4–6 leads (strongest research quality first)

**Note:** For Round 1 audit-led approach, the concept brief serves as internal evidence — it demonstrates we have a specific, considered view of what this business's website should do. It doesn't need to be a polished design mockup.

**Output:** 4–6 concept briefs in lead folders

---

## Phase 4 — Pitch Drafts (Days 10–12)

### Who: Mercury

**Action:** Draft outreach PITCH.md for 4–5 leads

**The pitch format for Round 1:**
- Lead with the audit offer (free, specific, no commitment)
- Hook: specific observation about their business (from research)
- Body: what we found in our brief review — specific, not generic
- CTA: "Would you be open to seeing what we found? No commitment."
- Subject: business name + "a quick observation"

**Output:** 4–5 pitch drafts, clearly marked DRAFT

---

## Phase 5 — Approval Queue (Day 12)

### Who: Nero

**Action:** Review 4–5 complete lead packages

**Decision per lead:** APPROVED / REJECTED / PARKED

**Cap:** Maximum 5 approved sends for Round 1. If more than 5 leads are approved, prioritise by score and defer the rest to Round 2.

**Output:** Up to 5 leads approved for outreach

---

## Phase 6 — First Outreach Sends (Days 13–17)

### Who: Mercury (on explicit Nero deployment go-ahead)

**Rules:**
- Send 1 per day maximum — stagger across the week
- Nero must separately approve deployment for each send (not just content approval)
- Suppression check confirmed before each send

**Sequence:**

- Day 14: Send 2
- Day 15: Send 3
