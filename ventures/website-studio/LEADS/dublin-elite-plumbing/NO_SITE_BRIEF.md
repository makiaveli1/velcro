# No-Site Brief — Dublin Elite Plumbing

**Slug:** `dublin-elite-plumbing`

**Audited by:** Scout

## Executive Summary

Dublin Elite Plumbing has a dominant Google Business Profile with 89 reviews at 4.9 stars but zero web presence. Customers find them on Google Maps, call directly, and likely never research further. A professional website would capture the 40–60% of potential customers who research online before committing — and convert more of the calls they already get into booked jobs.

---

- **Google Business Profile:** Excellent — 89 reviews, regular posts, active Q&A, photos of completed work
- **Facebook page:** Basic, infrequent posts, minimal engagement
- **Google Maps listing:** Dominant for "emergency plumber Dublin 12"
- **No website whatsoever**

## The Information Gap

A potential new customer researching "emergency plumber Dublin 12" will find Dublin Elite Plumbing on Google Maps. But when they try to:
- See examples of past work → nothing
- Understand pricing structure → nothing
- Build confidence in the business before calling → nothing
- Find an online booking option → nothing
- Check for specific services → nothing

They either call (if they feel confident) or move to the next result. Many will move on.

---

## Specific Opportunity

### The Problem
Emergency plumbing is a high-urgency, high-trust category. Customers in crisis need to feel immediate confidence. A Google Business Profile alone doesn't fill that gap — it's transactional, not relational.

### What a Website Would Do
1. **Convert Map Traffic to Bookings** — A site gives a professional landing point for every Google Maps click
2. **Pre-Qualify Emergency Calls** — Clear service pages reduce "is this the right plumber?" calls
3. **Showcase Work** — Photo gallery of completed jobs builds trust before the first conversation
4. **Capture Non-Urgent Enquiries** — Many people need a plumber for non-emergency work (bathroom renos, boiler installs) but have no way to research this business online
5. **Differentiate from Cowboy Trades** — A professional site signals legitimacy in a market flooded with unqualified operators

---

## Recommended Website Type

**Lead-generation site for a Dublin emergency plumber.**

Priority order:
1. Homepage — "24/7 Emergency Plumber Dublin" + phone CTA above the fold
2. Services — Emergency, Heating, Bathrooms, Installations
3. About / Why Us — Trust signals: years in business, certifications, insurance, 89 Google reviews highlight
4. Gallery — Before/after of completed work
5. Contact — Phone, form, map embed

**This is NOT a project portfolio site — it's a trust-and-conversion machine for a service business.**
